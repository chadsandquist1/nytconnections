# Lifetime Fitness Edina - 20 Week Workout Program

## Overview

This is a progressive 52-week workout program designed for Personized Fitness in Edina. Each week consists of 3 workouts, with each workout lasting 45 minutes (20 minutes cardio + 25 minutes strength training).

## Program Structure

- **Duration:** 52 weeks
- **Frequency:** 3 days per week
- **Workout Length:** 45 minutes per session
  - Cardio: 20 minutes
  - Strength Training: 25 minutes

## Equipment Used

- Dumbbells
- Exercise machines (chest press, leg press, lat pulldown, etc.)
- Treadmill
- StairStepper
- Bodyweight exercises (push-ups, sit-ups, planks, etc.)

## Weekly Split

- **Day 1:** Upper Body Focus (Chest, Shoulders, Arms)
- **Day 2:** Lower Body Focus (Legs, Glutes)
- **Day 3:** Full Body / Core & Conditioning

## Progressive Overload

The program progressively increases in intensity over the 20 weeks:

- **Weeks 1-4:** Foundation building, learning movements
- **Weeks 5-8:** Increased volume and intensity
- **Weeks 9-12:** Strength focus with heavier weights
- **Weeks 13-16:** Power and conditioning
- **Weeks 17-20:** Peak performance and endurance

Recovery weeks are built in at weeks 4, 8, 12, and 16 with lighter loads to allow for adaptation.

## Navigation

- Click **Next →** or **← Previous** buttons to move between weeks
- Use **arrow keys** (← →) on your keyboard for quick navigation
- Click "Back to Connections Game" to return to the main app

## Accessing the Program

**Online:** http://your-bucket-name.s3-website-us-east-1.amazonaws.com/workouts/

The workout program is hosted on AWS S3 alongside the NYT Connections game.

## Notes

- All exercises can be adjusted based on fitness level
- Rest 30-60 seconds between sets
- Focus on proper form over weight
- Stay hydrated throughout workouts
- Warm up before cardio and cool down after strength training
- Listen to your body and adjust intensity as needed

## Customization

To modify the workouts, edit `workouts.js` in this directory. The data structure is straightforward and each week contains 3 days with cardio and strength exercises.
